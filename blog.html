Our Certificate & Online Programs For 2022

With state-of-art tech, an unmatched alumni network,and faculty and staff who strive to meet learners where they are,High Tech Academy makes studying online all about connection and flexibility. Our alumni network is second to none,and you will experience it fully during your time at High Tech Academy. You will connect with professionals in your field through events, info sessions, and in-classroom discusiions that creat impactful growth opportunities. We view success as a profesional and personal pursuit and success only counts if it is achieved conscientiously. That is why we emphasize both relevant skills,and ethical decision making in every program. So you will learn to balance profitability,sustanability,and social responsibilty as you grow in your career.


We challenge studnets but flex when they need us to which is why we believe building skills could mean intense study sessions and pushing the limits, but if life gets in the way, we will understand. At High Tech, you will know your professors and academic advisors well enough to reach out and have a real conversation about what you need to be successful. High Tech Academy student Support Advisors are dedicated to student success. Your Student Support Advisor will always have your back. Whether that means arranging online college courses to coordinating study plans or simply checking in to see how things are going, you can be rest assured knowing that a dedicated advisor is keeping an eye out for you.


Your High Tech Career Services Advisor will get to know you, your career goals and aspirations. Well before graduation, your Career Services Advisor will work with you on things like developing a personal brand, polishing your resume, optimizing your LinkedIn profile and providing interview coaching when the time is right. At High Tech Academy, we know your education is more than just earning a degree. It is also about how you can potentially use your credentials to open new and exciting doors for your life.


Busy adults in online degree programs need time to study whenever they can, which is why we offer 24/7 live tutoring any time our students need it. We know that the flexibility of an online education is the only way that many people can earn a degree while supporting their family. You shouldn't have to choose between improving yourself and maintaining your regular obligations. We'll work to understand your situation and do our best to support your goals throughout your time at High Tech Academy.
